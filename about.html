About RS Aquatic

Crafting underwater worlds since 2009. We transform spaces with stunning aquatic ecosystems.

With over 15 years of experience in the aquarium industry, RS Aquatic has built a reputation as one of India’s most trusted aquarium solution providers. Our team consists of passionate aquascapers, aquarium designers, and experts who are committed to delivering the highest quality aquarium products and services to our clients.

We offer end-to-end solutions for aquarists, from designing custom aquarium setups and selecting the right aquatic plants to providing ongoing maintenance and cleaning services. Whether you're a hobbyist or a business owner looking to enhance your space, RS Aquatic brings nature’s beauty right into your home or office.
